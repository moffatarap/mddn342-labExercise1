Stargate SG1

Johnathan "Jack" O'Neill is a Colonel in the United States Air Force and the founding commanding officer of SG-1. He joined the Air Force when he was 18 years old and was assigned to the Special Forces unit. After retiring from the United States Air Force, he is called back to active duty by Major General George S. Hammond when the Stargate is unexpectedly activated and Cheyenne Mountain Complex.

Teal'c is a Jaffa and one of Earth's leading authorities on the Goa'uld, a race of beings that use other bodies as hosts. Teal'c has a golden tattoo on his forehead. This represents the fact that he once served the System Lord Apophis as First Prime. Like all Jaffa, Teal'c also carries an infant symbiote in his belly pouch that serves as an incubator for a larval Goa'uld.

Dr. Daniel Jackson is a brilliant archaeologist and a linguist who speaks multiple languages and holds doctorates in Archaeology, Anthropology, and Philology. He is an expert at translating Egyptian hieroglyphics and is part of the team headed up by Colonel Jack ONeil and comprised of Samantha Carter and Teal'c.

Samantha Carter is a United States Air Force captain who joins the SG-1 team under the command of Colonel Jack O'Neill. She is considered the Earth's leading expert on Stargate and other alien technology. Captain Carter believes she should have been part of the first team to go to Abydos with Colonel Jack O'Neill and Dr. Daniel Jackson.
